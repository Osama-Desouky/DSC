    About Us

    It was established by Cabinet Resolution No. (337) and is an organizationally linked center At the Saudi Royal Court and financially and administratively independent, the center aims to Supporting decision-making and improving its effectiveness and adequacy in various fields in The required time and the appropriate accuracy, through scientific mechanisms and means A process that ensures appropriate decision-making and implementation

    Our Goal

    The center aims to support and enhance the decision system in the Kingdom in all fields by issuing studies and reports that would support the decision-making process to be based on objectivity, evidence, evidence and big data.

    Our Vision

    To be a leading authority in providing opinion and advice to support the decision-making system.

    Our message

    Supporting the decision-making process and improving its effectiveness and adequacy in various fields at the required time and with appropriate accuracy, Through scientific and practical mechanisms and means that ensure appropriate decision-making and implementation.

    work fields

    providing information

    Contribute to providing the necessary information to the Council of Ministers, the Council of Political and Security Affairs, and the Council of Economic and Development Affairs.

    Monitoring and Analysis

    Research and analyze quantitative and qualitative information and monitor the most important internal and external events in order to conduct diagnostic studies and reference comparisons.

    Data collection and analysis

    Collecting, documenting, analyzing, processing and storing relevant data and information to serve the decision maker.

    Preparing future studies

    Study and analyze the impact of global trends on various topics and industries related to the Kingdom, and formulate recommendations that enable participation in the making and formulation of future events.

    Preparing development studies

    Preparing studies related to priority issues in terms of development and the obstacles, difficulties and problems they face, and making recommendations in this regard.

    Contribute to educating the public

    Contribute to educating and sensitizing public opinion about the decisions taken, the topics raised, and the issues raised
